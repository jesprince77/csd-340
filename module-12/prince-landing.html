I recently found that I have a love for painting. In 2019 I painted my first three acrylic paintings on canvas.

I have three saltwater aquarium. Two nano reefs, 13.5 gallon and 10 gallon along with a 1.5 gallon as a hospital tank. In my coral collection I have zoas, pulsating zenia, duncan and mushroom corals, along with clown, damsel and wrasse fish and a host of invertibrates.
